What is the WWF?

"The World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) is an international organization working on issues regarding the conservation, research and restoration of the environment. WWF was founded in 1961."

More about the WWF

"World Wildlife Fund works in nearly 100 countries to tackle the most pressing issues at the intersection of nature, people, and climate."

The origin of The WWF

"WWF was established in 1961 by a group of passionate and committed individuals who sought to secure the funding necessary to protect places and species that were threatened by human development.Edward Max Nicholson founded it."
